How to Play Quizter

Goal: Score the most points by answering questions correctly and quickly.
  • Each round has one question and a countdown timer.
  • Correct answers earn base points, and some games may also include a speed bonus.
  • Incorrect answers earn zero points.
  • Round-end graphics will flash Correct/Incorrect for quick feedback.
  • Use your one-time power-ups strategically when the host has power-ups enabled.
Power-ups (one use each per game):
  • Mix Master: Scrambles displayed answer text/order for other players.
  • Speed Searcher: Locks others out and gives you 60 seconds to answer.
  • Double Downer: Doubles your score for the round if you answer correctly.
  • Clone Commander: Copies the top round score to your round score.
  • Super Spliter: Reduces choices to one correct and one incorrect option.
  • Great Gambler: Multiplies round scores for answerers by a random factor (-1.0 to 3.0).
