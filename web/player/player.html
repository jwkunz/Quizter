How to Play Quizster

Goal: Score the most points by answering questions correctly and quickly.
  • Each round has one question and a countdown timer.
  • Correct answers earn base points, and some games may also include a speed bonus.
  • Incorrect answers earn zero points.
  • Round-end graphics will flash Correct/Incorrect for quick feedback.
  • Use your one-time power-ups strategically when the host has power-ups enabled.
Power-ups (one use each per game):
  • Mix Master: Scrambles displayed answer text and order for the other players this round.
  • Speed Searcher: Gives you an exclusive 60-second answer window and locks out the other players.
  • Double Downer: Doubles your score for this round if you answer correctly.
  • Clone Commander: Gives you the top round score earned by any player this round.
  • Super Spliter: Helps you by reducing your visible choices to one correct and one incorrect option.
  • Great Gambler: Applies a random round-score multiplier from -1.0 to 3.0.
